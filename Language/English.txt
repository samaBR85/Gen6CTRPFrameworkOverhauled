NOTE_ENEMY_HELPER :: Coach card for the enemy Pokemon - a companion to Display Enemy Stats.\n\nTOP: the foe's card (species, types, level, Nature with +/- stat, Hidden Power, Ability, held Item) and 'Hit it with' = the attack types that are super-effective on it (2x or 4x). Under the sprite, 'In Box' / 'Not in Box' tells you whether you already own this species in your PC boxes.\n\nDETAILS tab: what its Ability, Item and 4 Moves do, then two summaries -\n\nMATCHUP = the type chart for this foe. 'Resists' are types it takes reduced damage from (avoid them); 'Immune' are types that do 0 damage (never use). The super-effective side is the 'Hit it with' chips on the top screen.\n\nTIPS (the green lines) = how to play it, read from its real stats and moves. E.g. 'Strong special attacker - raise your Sp. Def' means it will hit you mostly with special moves; 'Carries a status move - bring a status cure' means it can inflict a status condition.\n\nCOMPARE tab: the foe's six stats (HP, Atk, Def, Sp.Atk, Sp.Def, Spe) lined up against your active Pokemon, side by side. Each value is the FULL total - base + IVs + EVs + nature + level - so it's a fair comparison; in every row the higher number shows green and the lower one is dimmed, with a Total at the bottom. A quick read on where you out-muscle the foe, and where it out-muscles you.\n\nITEMS tab: handy PokeMart consumables for this fight; tap or press A to add one to your bag (in PAY mode it costs money, FREE is free).\n\nUp/Down scroll Details (or move between items); L/R switch the enemy slot; X swaps tabs; B back; SELECT exit.\n\nWorks only while you are in a battle.
